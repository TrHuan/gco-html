Aladiex token offering

Road map

The new fundraising platform which invented by AladiEx, which belongs to the intellectual property of AladiEx, the first globally, will create a capital raising revolution.

  • 1. Multiple rounds

    Release ALADIN in multiple phases following the company's development

  • 2. KPIs

    In each development phase, the company has KPI’s suitable for each round and the company must meet the KPI from the previous round to be qualified to raise funds in the next round

  • 3. Freezing and Release

    The number of tokens owned by buyers will be frozen until the next round and be partially released in order to prevent price manipulation and to maintain a stable growth opportunity for the token value.

  • 4. Bonus Token

    Buyers in the previous round will be rewarded with a number of bonus tokens according to their holding proportion when the company raises funds in the next round

  • 5. Loyalty Program

    We are committed to reward up to 70% of the profits of the entire AladiEx Eco-system monthly by Smart contract, according to the purchaser's tokens held
