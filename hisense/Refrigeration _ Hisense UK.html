Total No Frost means no frost, anywhere – ever!

More advanced than ordinary frost-free, the innovative Total No Frost Technology from Hisense circulates cold air evenly throughout your fridge and freezer to prevent ice crystals from forming, so there’s no need to ever manually defrost.

More advanced than ordinary frost-free.

  • Intelligent and evenly distributed air system in both fridge & freezer.

  • No water or ice build up in fridge or freezer ever.

More accurate control of your fridge freezer’s features.

Take complete and accurate control over your fridge freezer’s features with a clear, easy to reach and simple to use LED touch control panel, which allows you to quickly select the desired temperature for each compartment.

Perfectly chilled water and fresh ice, whenever you want it.

Get perfectly cooled water and fresh crushed or cubed ice at the touch of a button with a plumbed water and ice dispenser. And because the appliance is permanently connected to your mains water supply, there’s no refilling required.
